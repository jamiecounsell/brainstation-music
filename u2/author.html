I began my Web / Mobile Application development viewing a lot of online educational videos and reading many books on the various technologies including: HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL, PHP, Swift, Python. Within the past couple of years I worked on producing a business application for the iOS App Store and an accompanying web site. Beginning in the Fall of 2017 I enrolled in the Remote Web Development (Online) course offered by Brainstation in Toronto, to further strengthen my Web Skills.

About Me

I am a huge Stevie Wonder fan and I created this site out of pure respect of this music legend and icon. I listen to Stevie songs ALL THE TIME, and my kids love his music too. My influence perhaps? Yes.

I attended his Songs in the Key of Life concert back in 2014 when I was 8 months pregnant. I was dancing and so was my first born in my belly! Stevie, your music is timeless and your voice is absolutely incredible.

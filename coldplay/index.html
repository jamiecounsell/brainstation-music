Coldplay's fan page

Coldplay, formed in 1996 is a British rock band and was started by lead guitarist Jonny Buckland and lead vocalist and keyboardist Chris Martin during their time in University College London. They started with the name Pectoralz, later changing it into Starfish and finally Coldplay. Guy Berryman, joined the group as a bassist in 1997 and Will Champion as the drummer later.

About The Author: Charley Ballantyne

Photo: Maurizio Von Flotow

Charley grew up in London, Ontario, but he's always been destined for the west coast. Moving west in 2007 to attend UVic Charley left his friends and family behind to embark on a mission to ski, surf and experience all that was possible in one of the most beautiful areas in the world. It's been a decade and Charley is still firmly rooted in this blessed country.

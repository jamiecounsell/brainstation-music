ABOUT ME

My name is Lily Zazalak, I am a 20 year old student living in beautiful British Columbia. I was born in Kitimat BC, but grew up on Salt Spring Island BC. The Island is one of the most popular of the gulf islands here on the west coast. Its a great little town full of artists and very spiritual hippies. I really love being outside, so I was extremly lucky to growing up on an island with beautiful scenery. FleedWood Mac is one of my favourite bands of all time. They remind me of spending time with family and friends in my home town. I'm creating this website as a project for school. I hope that you like it!
